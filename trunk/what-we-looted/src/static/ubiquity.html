Simply click on "Subscribe" button on the bar above and enjoy :)

To use, press ALT+SPACE (or the combination you set for your Ubiquity), write tibia, and then you will see your options.

You can use tab key, to autocomplete command.

While you type in character name, or wiki subject, ubiquity will try to load the preview.

If you wish to go to the page, simply hit enter, and the proper page will be loaded.

There are 3 commands avaiable now:
  • tibia-character [character]- show tiba character page
  • tibia-wiki [subject] - show Tibia Wiki page
  • tibia-online [character] - show character online counter from http://www.pskonejott.com
